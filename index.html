Raymond VanTilburg

I found Science Fiction Conventions in the early 80's. That's were I started producing t-shirts with my art and hand airbrushing each one. Which evolved into OffWorld Designs, doing 40-50 shows a year, selling t-shirts. I have been designing and producing shirts for several of the conventions including, Norwescon, Windycon, Arisia, Worldcons: Interaction, Anticipation, Chicon 7, Loncon 3, Sasquan, MidAmeriCon II, and Dublin and many others. Over the years I have been honored to be the Artist Guest and have received awards at several conventions. My personal art was always squeezed in when there were a few minutes. The past several years

I have been doing quite a bit of personal art and showing at more conventions. In 2020 I opened an Etsy store since there weren't going to be any conventions to sell art at for at least a year or two. Working primarily in Graphite, Acrylics, & Watercolor. Usually a mixed media of these and other media. The bulk of my art is Fantasy, Science Fiction oriented landscapes spacescapes, surrealistic & imaginary images. Really a wide range of subjects and styles probably from my years of designing for convention shirts.
